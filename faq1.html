ABOUT OUR SERVICE

Motoserv is a technology-driven multi-brand automotive service provider in India. Motoserv provides vehicle repairs, inspections, maintenance and more than 150 services to vehicle owners. We are solv the problem of long waiting time, transparency and quality of car repair services in India. Our highly experienced & expert technicians provide services in Guwahati city. With our slogan "Mechanic at your Doorstep," we seek to service vehicles of various brands anytime, anywhere using technology. Our mobile model and cloud-based platform replace the physical infrastructure of garages, allowing us to charge vehicle owners less and attract top-notch mechanics who can earn much better than in the traditional market. Through our website and mobile app, you can get quotes, book services, and get advice from experienced mechanics. With your free online account, you can also view the maintenance schedule and track the service history of your vehicle.

In most cases, we will give you an instant quote. You will see the breakdown of labor fees and parts costs. If an instant price is not available, we will send you a quote within one business day, often sooner. You can book an appointment by selecting a time that is convenient for you. You will be matched with a mechanic who has the necessary expertise to perform the work on your car. Only customers who have completed an appointment with us can write a review - this guarantees that all reviews are from real customers. Motoserv will perform the service at the convenience of your home or office. We bill your credit card, Debit Card, and Cash on Service after the work is done. You can access your service report and receipt in your online account. All services are backed by our 3 -month / 1000-Km (whichever comes first) warranty.
